"Cyberbullying has become a significant problem in the digital age, with social media platforms and online communities providing new avenues for harassment and abuse. Victims of cyberbullying can experience emotional distress, depression, and anxiety, and the effects can be long-lasting. Addressing cyberbullying requires a multi-faceted approach, including better education, stronger online platform policies, and legal frameworks to protect victims. Promoting kindness, respect, and responsible online behavior is key to reducing cyberbullying and ensuring that the internet remains a safe space for everyone."
