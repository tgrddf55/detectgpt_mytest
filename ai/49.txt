The rise of remote work has fundamentally changed the way businesses operate and how employees approach their careers. With advancements in communication technologies, such as video conferencing and collaboration platforms, employees can work from anywhere in the world. This shift has allowed businesses to tap into a global talent pool, enabling them to hire the best candidates regardless of location. However, remote work also comes with challenges, such as maintaining team cohesion, managing productivity, and ensuring employees' well-being. As remote work becomes more common, companies are finding new ways to adapt their management practices to support distributed teams.
