"Education systems worldwide are undergoing significant changes, with digital learning and remote education becoming increasingly popular. The COVID-19 pandemic accelerated this shift, pushing schools and universities to adopt online platforms and virtual classrooms. While online education offers flexibility and convenience, it also presents challenges such as accessibility, engagement, and digital literacy. To ensure that all students benefit from digital learning, it is essential to invest in technology, training, and support systems that address these barriers and provide an equitable educational experience."
