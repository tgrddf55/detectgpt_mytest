The growing popularity of video streaming platforms has changed the entertainment industry, offering consumers more options for watching movies, TV shows, and live events. Services like Netflix, Hulu, and Disney+ have revolutionized the way people access content, providing on-demand streaming and original programming. The shift towards digital streaming has disrupted traditional media outlets, such as cable TV, and led to a decline in physical media formats like DVDs. As video streaming continues to grow, it is expected to further reshape the entertainment landscape, offering new opportunities for creators and content distributors.
